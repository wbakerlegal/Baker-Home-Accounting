Privacy Policy

This website (q͜ʘ, Baker Home Accounting; the “Software”) uses data stored in a Google Sheets spreadsheet associated with your Google account to help you keep a basic accounting journal and view the information you compile in the journal.

The Software’s use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

This policy will be updated to reflect any changes to what the Software does with your data, and the Software will prominently notify you of any such update.

Access of Your Data

As you use the Software, the Software retrieves data (the “Spreadsheet Data”) from a Google Sheets spreadsheet either (a) ‘owned’ by your Google account or (b) to which you have access (the “Linked Spreadsheet”).

The Spreadsheet Data consists of:

  1. the contents of the sheets titled “Journal,” “Account List,” and “Recurring Entries” in the Linked Spreadsheet;
  2. basic properties of the Linked Spreadsheet including its URL and the title and ID of the Linked Spreadsheet and of its component sheets.

If the Linked Spreadsheet has sheets with titles other than those listed above, the Software does not access the contents of those other sheets.

During use of the Software, data in the “Journal,” “Account List,” and “Recurring Entries” sheets of the Linked Spreadsheet may be rearranged or deleted without warning.

When you sign in, the Software receives a “Token” from Google that allows the Software to use Google APIs to access your data for an amount of time specified in the Token and only for the scope of your data specified in the Token. The Token contains no information about your Google account. The Software requests the minimum access scope available that allows it to create, see, and edit a Linked Spreadsheet.

Use of Your Data

The Software uses the Spreadsheet Data:

  • to present accounting-related summaries and reports derived from the data, and
  • to provide interfaces for you to modify the Spreadsheet Data.

The Software runs in your device’s web browser. The Software’s host serves static files and does not receive or process any Spreadsheet Data or any other data that may exist through the Software’s use of Google APIs (collectively, your “Data from Google”). The Software does not send any of your Data from Google to any network endpoints other than your device and those provided in the applicable Google APIs.

The Software makes no use of your Data from Google other than as detailed in this policy.

Storage of Your Data

The Software stores your data in (1) a Google Sheets spreadsheet and (2) your device’s web browser (specifically, through the browser’s Web Storage API).

The section “Access of Your Data,” above, details what the Software stores in the Google Sheets spreadsheet.

The Software allows you full control over whether the Spreadsheet Data and the sign-in Token are stored on your device through options in the General Setup area of the Software.

As a security procedure for the data stored on your device, you can remove any of the Data from Google that the Software stores on your device by using the controls in the General Setup area of the Software. By default, only the Linked Spreadsheet’s title and ID are stored on your device. The Software does not remove your Data from Google from your device until you direct it to do so. If you do use the Software to store data on your device, that data inherently is only as secure, private, and confidential as you keep your device.

Sharing of Your Data

Neither the Software, nor its owner, nor any person affiliated therewith, will share your data with any other person. As detailed elsewhere in this policy, your use of the Software does not allow the Software’s owner, developer, or host server even to access any of your Data from Google.

This policy does not apply to any sharing of your data that may occur other than through your use of the Software. If you are the ‘owner’ of the Linked Spreadsheet in Google Sheets, you are responsible for controlling access to the Linked Spreadsheet. The Software does not give you any control over who can access a Linked Spreadsheet.
